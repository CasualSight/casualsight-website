Graphic Designer (Multidisciplinary)

Location: Hong Kong and Philippines or fully remote or hybrid

Job Type: Full-time or Freelance

Job Summary

We’re looking for a creative all-rounder—a Graphic Designer who can do more than just make things look good. You’ll bring a guitar pedal brand aesthetic to life across platforms, combining visual storytelling with digital craftsmanship. This role is perfect for a versatile designer who’s fluent in branding, layout, image rendering, video editing, and front-end website design. If you have a deep appreciation for music, gear, and creative culture—especially within the world of guitar pedals, synths, or analog sound—we want to work with you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create consistent, compelling visual content across print, web, and product media.
  • Develop and manage brand assets, style guides, and marketing collateral.
  • Design and maintain front-end web layouts (HTML/CSS; basic JS a plus) using modern frameworks or builders.
  • Edit and produce short-form videos (product demos, social content, artist features).
  • Handle photo retouching, image rendering, and motion graphics when needed.
  • Collaborate with marketing and engineering teams to bring creative ideas to life.
  • Contribute to product packaging, manuals, merch, and promotional materials.
  • Help evolve the visual language as the brand grows.

Required Qualifications

  • Strong portfolio showcasing branding, digital design, and multimedia work.
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere, After Effects) or similar tools.
  • Front-end design skills (HTML/CSS; experience with WordPress, Webflow, or Framer is a plus).
  • Experience with video editing and post-production workflows.
  • Good visual instincts for typography, composition, and color.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects with attention to detail and deadlines.

Preferred Qualifications

  • A passion for music, audio gear, or analog electronics.
  • Experience designing for boutique brands or independent creative products.
  • Knowledge of UI/UX best practices.
  • Familiarity with 3D rendering tools (e.g., Blender, Figma mockups, or KeyShot).
  • Comfortable in a collaborative, cross-disciplinary creative environment.

To Apply

Please send your resume, portfolio, and a short cover letter explaining your experience and connection to music, sound, or creative storytelling to info@casualsight.com.
